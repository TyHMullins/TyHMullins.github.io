About Me

  • Who I Am

    Hi, I’m Ty Mullins, a college student majoring in Construction Management with a passion for both real estate and the construction industry. I’m currently working on obtaining my Affiliate Broker License, which will allow me to work as a real estate agent while I pursue my studies. My long-term goals include becoming a licensed contractor and real estate developer, combining my skills and experience in construction with my expertise in real estate.

  • My Mission

    My mission is to help individuals and businesses achieve their real estate and construction goals with professionalism, integrity, and a client-focused approach. Whether you're looking for your next home or investment property, or you're developing a new project, I’m here to guide you every step of the way. My goal is to create lasting relationships by delivering results that exceed expectations.

  • My Story

    From a young age, I’ve been fascinated by both real estate and construction. Watching homes and buildings come to life from blueprints to finished structures inspired me to pursue a career where I could merge these two passions. As a construction management major, I’ve gained hands-on experience in project planning, budgeting, and coordinating efforts across teams to ensure that projects are completed efficiently and to the highest standards. Now, as I prepare to launch my real estate career, I’m excited to help clients find their perfect property while applying my in-depth knowledge of construction and development.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of properties do you specialize in?

I specialize in residential properties, including single-family homes, condos, and townhouses. As a licensed affiliate broker, I focus on helping first-time homebuyers, families, and individuals find the perfect home that meets their needs and budget. In the future, I plan to expand into commercial real estate and real estate development.

How do I start the home-buying process?

The first step is to determine your budget and secure pre-approval for a mortgage, which will give you a clear idea of how much you can afford. After that, I can help you find properties that match your criteria, schedule viewings, and guide you through the entire process from making an offer to closing the deal.

How do you determine the value of a home I want to sell?

I conduct a thorough Comparative Market Analysis (CMA) that compares your property to recently sold homes in the area. I look at factors such as location, size, condition, and unique features to determine a competitive listing price that will attract buyers and maximize your return.

What services do you offer as a real estate agent?

I offer a full range of real estate services including: Helping buyers find and purchase their dream homes, Assiting sellers with listing and selling their properties, Negotiating the best deal on behalf of my clients, Providing real estate market insights and updates, Offering guidance on mortgage options and financing

Can you help with real estate investments?

Yes! If you're interested in real estate investing, I can help you identify promising investment properties, whether it's residential rental properties or potential flips. My background in construction management also gives me unique insights into property condition and renovation costs.

How do I stay updated on your projects and listings?

You can follow me on [LinkedIn/Instagram/Facebook], where I post regular updates on new listings, construction projects, and helpful real estate tips. Additionally, you can sign up for my email newsletter for more detailed updates.
